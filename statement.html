STATEMENT

Problem Statement

We have identified a gap in the currentPhilippine market where there are nocompanies focused on crypto assetmanagement for investors who wish to have anexposure in the crypto space but do not havethe necessary time or knowledge and skills for it.

We will be filling this gap by providing a servicethat provides oversight, advice, and assistancewith managing portfolios of crypto assets on aprofessional level as an ongoing service for clients.

Marketing & Client Acquisition Strategies

In-house Team

We will form a team of professionals who have been working in the Financial Services Industry who have the necessary knowledge and experience.

Independent Advisors

We will tap independent advisors such as insurance advisors and real estate brokers to get affiliated as an independent advisor.

Crypto Literacy

We will organize crypto literacy seminars and courses in order to funnel-in potential investors.

Partnerships

We will seek to get partnerships with institutions who already have investors that they give service to, e.g banks, investment companies, corporations.
