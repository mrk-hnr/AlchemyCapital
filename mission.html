MISSION

Alchemy Capital will provide a way for investors who want to invest in the cryptocurrency space but do not want the hassle of managing their own portfolio, to get exposure to high-risk/highreward assets such as Bitcoin and Ethereum without having to manage it themselves.

To become a leading player in this market by providing a onestop shop for crypto asset management services

To make crypto investments accessible and understandable for new users, as well as experienced investors.

To introduce convenient ways of investing in the Crypto space through ETPs and Crypto Index Funds

To introduce ways on how to profit from Blockchain, Decentralised Finance, and Web 3
