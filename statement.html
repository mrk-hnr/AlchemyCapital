Solution/Strategy

We will provide professional financial advisory and portfolio management services to individuals and companies who wish to diversify their investments into the Blockchain and Crypto Space through the following avenues:

  • Separately managed portfolios for high net worth individuals and companies.
  • Index funds and ETPs (Exchange Traded Funds) for retail investors.

Market Analysis

Cryptocurrency markets are booming. In 2023, the total market capitalization grew to $ 1 Trillion USD. We believe that this market has room for growth because there are many people who are interested in investing but don't know how or where to start. Crypto investment management is a multi-billion dollar industry that is growing at an exponential rate. There are many players in this space in the US, EU and other parts of the world, but it has not yet been fully explored in the Philippines. This is an opportunity for a company to gain an early foothold in the market.

Marketing & Client Acquisition Strategies

In-House Team

We will form a team of professionals who have been working in the Financial Services Industry who have the necessary knowledge and experience.

Independent Advisors

We will tap independent advisors such as insurance advisors and real estate brokers to get affiliated as an independent advisor.

Partnerships

We will seek to get partnerships with institutions who already have investors that they give service to, e.g banks, investment companies, corporations.

Crypto Literacy

We will organize crypto literacy seminars and courses in order to funnel-in potential investors

Data at a Glance

In the Philippines, there is a lack of regulation when it comes to cryptocurrencies as well as asset management services, which makes it difficult for investors to trust any person or group offering these services. This means there's an opportunity for us to step into this market as Finance Professionals and provide top quality service with minimal risk for our clients.
